END DIABETES

My motivation behind this site is to combined all of the different resources that I have come across in my journey towards a heathlier life style.

I have come to realize that Diabetes, a problem with blood sugar and insulin resistance, has become an epidemic in America and the world in general. I have personally been affected by this disease, both of my parents are diabetics, both of my grand fathers where eventually killed by this awful condition that can be prevented in many cases.

I believe that being healthy is a very general term and what constitutes are a healthy life style is a very subjective topic. What works for some does not work for others. Everyone has a different body type and different needs that have to be meet to achieve optimal health. I hope that I can show a different side of health that will possibly encourage people to think differently about what they have come to believe over the past 30 or so years.
